If you would like to give your special someone a basket full of candy, we can put one together for you.  You can do it by theme (nostalgic candy, movie theater boxes of candy, kids’ candies, etc.), or pick the person’s favorites.  We put it all in a basket covered in cellophane and tied with colorful ribbon. Include a Sweet Street Gift Card for a special treat. Prices vary, depending on the candy chosen. (Please give us 2-hour notice for one and 24-hour notice for additional baskets.)
