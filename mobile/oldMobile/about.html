    About Sweet Street

    Sweet Street is a fun, interactive candy store in the Danville Clock Tower!

    Sweet Street is a family-owned business run by Didi Reed. Didi loves children, and has four of her own! On December 24, 2010 Didi bought Sweet Street with the hopes of bringing a fun, child-friendly store to downtown Danville.

    Didi and her husband Billy are local Danville residents. They have four children, Krystia, Tory, T.J. and Jet who all attended San Ramon Valley High School. Didi has been very involved with her children's schools - she was PTA President, School Board Liaison, and Education Foundation Representative. She has also been involved in her children's extracurricular activities, including: basketball, football, softball, soccer, and dance.

    We hope you can stop by and say hi!
