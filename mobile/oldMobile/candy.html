Candy Selection

At Sweet Street, we strive to bring you a wide variety of candy at reasonable prices. We have the standard candy bars and retro nostalgic candy! We have theater boxes and candy in bulk! We have chocolates and truffles for the sophisticated and novelty candy for the fun-spirited! We have licorice, taffy, lollipops, and Jelly Beans! We even have sugar-free and other health-conscious options!

Sweet Street also has candy for every occasion, party, and gift! For weddings & color-themed parties, we can help you find candies to match your color scheme. We have seasonal candies for holliday parties and gifts. Our talented Hosts are happy to help you develop a list of candies appropriate for your theme or interest! And make sure to check out our Party page for more information regarding our party options!

Stop by the store today to see what we have or click here to see a list of the products we typically have! If we do not have an item that you are interested in, please let us know and we will try to order it for you!
